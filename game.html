How to Play

An otome experience inspired by Bungou Stray Dogs — as if the anime were a novel.

What is Page 0?

You experience reimagined moments from the anime as an otome visual novel. Navigate the world of the Armed Detective Agency and build bonds with characters through your choices.

Advancing the story

  • Click or tap the dialog box to advance to the next line.
  • Clicking while text is still typing will instantly complete the current line.
  • Use Space / Enter / → on keyboard. ← goes back one scene.
  • Press A to toggle Auto mode — the story advances automatically.

Choices & Affection

  • Most choices offer 3 options: one increases affection ❤️, one is neutral, one decreases it 💔.
  • The gauges on the left track your relationship with each love interest in real time.
  • Fill a gauge to 100% to unlock that character's ending.
  • Multiple endings exist — your choices shape the story.

Chapter types

  • Main chapters — the core story. Play in order.
  • Special scenes — optional. Can earn bonus affection points.
  • Bonus scenes — unlocked by high affection. Contain exclusive romantic CGs and moments.

Saving

  • Tap the 💾 icon to quick-save at any time.
  • You'll always be asked to save before leaving.
  • Progress is stored locally in your browser.
